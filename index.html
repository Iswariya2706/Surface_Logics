01. The Challenge

The Urban Water Crisis

Despite adequate rainfall, cities are steadily depleting their aquifers as construction practices ignore natural water infiltration and recharge dynamics, impacting not just urban areas, but entire shared water systems.

Impervious & Uninformed Design

Building layouts are created without considering soil, slope, and water flow, leading to excessive concretization that blocks natural groundwater recharge and increases runoff.

Ineffective, Generic RWH Systems

Rainwater harvesting is treated as a compliance checkbox rather than a site-specific engineered solution, resulting in poor recharge despite sufficient rainfall.

Urban Runoff, Rural Impact

Uncaptured rainwater is lost through drainage systems, weakening shared aquifers—directly affecting farmers by reducing borewell reliability and increasing long-term water stress.

02. The Platform

Intelligent Water Resilience Solution

An end-to-end AI-driven framework that transforms any plot into a water-optimized, compliance-ready development—seamlessly integrating environmental intelligence with architectural design.

01

Soil–Concrete Ratio Optimizer (SCRO)

A proprietary engine that performs site-specific hydrological analysis using geospatial and environmental parameters to determine the optimal balance between built and permeable spaces. It recommends scientifically designed water recharge strategies with precise sizing and placement, ensuring effective groundwater absorption based on real-world conditions.

02

AI Layout Generator

An intelligent design system that automatically generates fully editable 2D building layouts, combining user requirements with hydrological constraints and municipal planning rules. The result is optimized spatial planning that enhances water recharge while preserving usability, aesthetics, and property value.

03

Automated Municipal Compliance

Eliminates manual drafting delays by converting designs directly into structured, municipal-compliant .dxf files, ready for approval workflows on platforms like AutoDCR—ensuring faster approvals with minimal rework.
